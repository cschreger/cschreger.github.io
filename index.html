ABOUT ME

Professional

I’m a full stack developer with a lifelong passion for creative problem-solving. As a developer, what motivates me is finding and implementing solutions to everyday problems both large and small. I believe that data is the first key to understanding and tackling an issue, and thus my focus revolves around both storing backend data efficiently, figuring out the story that the data is telling, and then presenting this data through a clean and beautiful frontend user experience.

Much of my work is inspired by my personal hobbies and passions - such as puzzles, reading, the outdoors, movies, and human psychology/philosophy. When not working, you can often find me talking ad nauseam about one of the aforementioned topics.
